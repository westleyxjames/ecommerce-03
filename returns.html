Returns & Exchanges

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to love your Velmora purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unworn, unwashed, undamaged, and in original condition with all tags attached.

Eligible Items

Most items purchased from Velmora can be returned within 30 days. However, the following items are final sale and cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Worn, washed, or altered items
  • Items without original tags
  • Intimate apparel and undergarments
  • Gift cards
  • Sale items marked as final sale

How to Return

Step 1: Contact Us

Email returns@velmora.com with your order number and items you wish to return. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours.

Step 2: Pack Your Items

Place items in their original packaging if possible. Include a copy of your order confirmation or packing slip.

Step 3: Ship It Back

Attach the prepaid return label to your package and drop it off at any USPS location.

Step 4: Get Your Refund

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Return Shipping

Free Returns within Continental US: We provide a prepaid return label at no cost for returns within the continental United States.

Alaska, Hawaii, and US Territories: Return shipping costs will be deducted from your refund. We recommend using a tracked shipping method.

International Orders: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using an insured, trackable shipping method.

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color within 30 days of delivery. To exchange an item:

  1. Contact us at exchanges@velmora.com
  2. Return your original item using our prepaid label
  3. We'll ship your exchange item within 2 business days of receiving your return

Note: Exchanges are subject to availability. If your desired size/color is out of stock, we'll issue a refund instead.

Refund Processing

  • Inspection: Returns are inspected within 2 business days of arrival
  • Approval: You'll receive an email confirmation once your return is approved
  • Refund: Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • COD Orders: For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds will be issued via check or bank transfer

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@velmora.com with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund including original shipping charges.

Important: Claims for damaged or defective items must be made within 7 days of delivery.

Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days at support@velmora.com. We'll arrange for the correct item to be sent to you at no additional cost and provide a prepaid label to return the incorrect item.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund within 14 days of our confirmation email:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company (it may take time before refund is posted)
  • Contact your bank (processing time may vary)
  • If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, contact us at support@velmora.com

Sale Items

Regular sale items can be returned unless marked as "Final Sale." All clearance items marked as final sale are not eligible for return or exchange.

Store Credit

If you prefer store credit instead of a refund, please let us know when initiating your return. Store credit never expires and can be used on any future purchase.
