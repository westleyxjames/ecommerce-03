Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to all 50 US states, including:

  • Continental US: Standard shipping rates apply
  • Alaska & Hawaii: Additional shipping fees may apply due to distance
  • US Territories: Contact us for shipping rates and availability

We do not currently ship internationally. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Order Processing

  • Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding holidays
  • Orders placed before 12pm EST ship the same business day
  • Orders placed after 12pm EST ship the next business day
  • Weekend orders begin processing on Monday
  • Processing may take 1-2 business days during peak seasons

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with:

  • Tracking number
  • Link to track your package
  • Estimated delivery date

You can track your order 24/7 through the carrier's website. Please allow 24 hours after shipment for tracking information to update.

Delivery

Signature Requirements

Orders over $500 require an adult signature upon delivery. If no one is available to sign, the carrier will leave a notice with instructions for pickup or redelivery.

Safe Place Delivery

If you won't be home, you can leave delivery instructions in the order notes during checkout. We're not responsible for items left in unsecured locations.

PO Boxes

We can ship to PO Boxes via USPS only. Express shipping is not available for PO Box addresses.

Shipping Restrictions

Please note that we cannot deliver to:

  • Freight forwarders or package consolidators
  • Military APO/FPO addresses (we're working on this!)
  • International addresses

Multiple Item Orders

Most orders ship together in one package. However, if items are coming from different warehouses, you may receive multiple shipments. You'll receive separate tracking numbers for each package at no additional cost.

Shipping Delays

While rare, shipments may be delayed due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Carrier delays
  • Peak holiday seasons
  • Incorrect shipping address
  • Customs delays (for territories)

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please contact us at support@velmora.com.

Lost or Stolen Packages

If tracking shows your package was delivered but you haven't received it:

  1. Check with neighbors or household members
  2. Look around your delivery location (porch, garage, etc.)
  3. Contact the shipping carrier
  4. Wait 24 hours (sometimes packages are marked delivered before arrival)
  5. Contact us at support@velmora.com if the package doesn't arrive

We're not responsible for stolen packages, but we'll work with you to resolve the issue.

Incorrect Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct during checkout. If you realize there's an error:

  • Before Shipping: Contact us immediately to update the address
  • After Shipping: Contact the carrier directly to request an address change

We're not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

Holiday Shipping

During major holidays, please order early to ensure timely delivery. We recommend:

  • Standard Shipping: Order 2 weeks before the holiday
  • Express Shipping: Order 1 week before the holiday

Holiday shipping cutoff dates will be posted on our homepage.
